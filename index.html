🏡 Community Progress Report

← Back to Home

🔒 Infrastructure, Property Documentation & Security Improvements

  • Helped 800+ residents get BBMP e-KHATA for their properties
  • Cleared property tax dues for the clubhouse
  • Installed boom barriers to control vehicle entry
  • Marked parking slots and organized visitor parking
  • Started MyGate system with UNISERVICE for safe and easy gate control
  • Updated owner, maid, and staff records in MyGate and ADDA systems
  • Added clear block-wise signs to help visitors and delivery people find their way
  • Installed extra CCTV cameras to improve security
  • Improved how we track and solve issues, so problems get fixed faster

🌱 Facilities & Sustainability Upgrades

  • Renovated clubhouse, toilets, and gym; renewed maintenance contracts
  • Installed comfortable backrest benches for senior citizens.
  • Fixed the fly problem around H and I blocks by improving wet waste management
  • Fixed terrace leaks and connected STP water to avoid wastage
  • Added borewells, cleaned rainwater systems, and repaired drainage to stop flooding
  • Solved an 8-year-old water backflow issue with in-house help, saving costs
  • Made the mini-forest zone cleaner and more inviting, encouraging more people to visit
  • Installed handrails at important steps for the safety of senior citizens
  • Set up drip irrigation in garden areas to water plants automatically and reduce manual work

💰 Smart Financial Management

  • Collected old unpaid rent from commercial tenants like Reliance, Apollo, and GoGlam
  • Reduced unpaid association dues from 10–12% to 2–3% by applying strict follow-ups
  • Created new income sources: flea markets, dosa stalls, and display boards
  • Started earning from scrap disposal
  • Made the approval process for spending more transparent and organized
  • Solved a long-standing legal case through friendly dialogue, and all dues were cleared
  • Set up a Ramdev Medical shop in the old ATM space, providing medicines and rental income

🤝 Civic Work & Public Services

  • Solved the long-standing underground drainage (UGD) connection problem by working with government agencies
  • Followed up regularly with local officials to improve roads and add more streetlights

🚀 Our Vision for the Next Term

In the last term, we worked hard to solve important legacy issues without adding extra cost to residents. With your trust, here's what we plan to do next:

🔧 Service & Maintenance

  • Make Uniservice respond faster and solve problems more quickly
  • Talk to the lift company to fix repeated breakdowns

🚗 Security & Smart Solutions

  • Improve car park security using cameras and tech solutions
  • Raise the compound wall for better safety and appearance
  • Install EV charging points at low cost, so electric vehicles can be supported

🏢 Clubhouse Improvements

  • Fix the AC in the clubhouse
  • Repair or replace old gym machines
  • Paint and refresh the party hall
  • Set up simple software for clubhouse access and hall bookings

📚 Library Revamp

  • Fix and organize the library so it can be useful again

🎨 Looks & Community Feel

  • Repair damaged ceilings on ground floors of blocks
  • Paint pergola structures at entrances to make them look nicer

🎉 Celebrations & Events

  • Take feedback from residents on how they would like to contribute to festivals like Ganapathy Habba, Rajyotsava, and Independence Day. Streamline the contribution process and accounting.
  • Make sure celebrations and flea markets are not too noisy or crowded
  • Create simple rules to keep events fun and respectful for everyone
